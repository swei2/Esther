Introduction

Many people might curious that what can AI do in education.
In general, people think education should be connected to human being and cannot use the technology or other resources and systems to replace it.
According to Li, from the perspective in education, learning and teaching should be the interaction between the instructors and the students. However, the current trend of the teaching mode, instructors are not teaching by students’ acknowledge abilities but they teach by the average of the acknowledge abilities from students. He stated that if the situation like this do not change, it will occur the obstacles between the instructors and the students.
Squirrel AI Learning develop the AI system which establish the nanoscale knowledge map and the MCM system to examine the students’ learning ability and the quality. This can combine with the teaching materials and the students’ knowledge levels. From this example, it shows that AI education is not to replace the instructors or change the current teaching environment but to improve and adapt to various situations.

Current Use

According to Sani, T, using the machines to determine the English level is the power and success of AI.
Currently machine is using machines to learn ML to calculate, when it provides the correct result is very incredible.
Another example for using AI in education field was in Carnegie training.
In this company, it provides a lot of personal lessons by using AI system. It can provide individual instruction, testing, and response.
By using AI, students can customize their classes that suit for them. Students can also use AI at home and obtain the worldwide classes and training programs.
AI can provide the opportunity to the disabilities students and provide the path for them to make a change.
AI can also detect their native language and do the translate or the subtitles input.

Security Aspects

In 2017, global has increased 963 billion USD with the growth rate of 8% on security budget of internet and database.
It was two times budget for the past two years. It shows that people are getting more and more concern of the security of Internet, such as antivirus, intrusion detection and prevention system. The safety of the Internet or the database is related to AI so that it is important to protect the personal data from the beginning of the AI system.
The informative security has the huge development on offices.
According to Kim, considering organizational variables, analyze and fit the standard of security.
Developing the model and the organizational variables and analyze its certification.
People should focus on the profile not to be exposed to the public and not to make the robots to replace the human thoroughly. Also, the robot should not consume the exact same human thoughts and intelligence.
If the robots have the exact same behavior and thoughts like human, the robots might out of control and will make the unreversible mistake.

Ethical and Social Implications

Usually people afraid of AI because they assume it will behave beyond the human being and out of control.
It is still an invisible ethic that people cannot invent the AI robot that can replace human’s knowledge or duplicate a person.
Especially big data is the important part of AI, such as personal information, and the life between the private and the public.
Nowadays, people require the technology should be humanely.
I am totally agreed with the quote from Ethics at the Bedside of the Digital Revolution,
“With the digitalization of the world, human exchanges have an essential ethical dimension because they are always conducted within the framework of a relationship with others, whose complexity is unsolvable.”
People should be aware of humanely because the temperature between human is the most precious thing in the world.
